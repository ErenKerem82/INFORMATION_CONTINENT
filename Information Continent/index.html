How to write "Article"

  • An article is a paper written to defend and prove an opinion, an opinion, on any subject. It is published in newspapers and magazines.Articles written to explain a fact, to put forward opinions and thoughts on a subject or to defend and support a thesis are also called "articles".Articles are written to prove a fact. Scientific data are used in the articles.

  • When writing articles, prefer plain language and never use 'I' language.Try not to allow contradiction. Pay attention to source reliability, don't use sources you don't trust

  • Avoid sentence repetition, do not make narrative deterioration.Collect the resources you use in the bibliography.

  • Pay attention to the article rules of the institution where you write the article. Line spacing, font, font size...Be sure to tie your article to a conclusion.

Why is it important to write an article?

  • Because the important thing in this type of text is to present a piece of information or thought in an accurate and understandable way.One of the most important types of scientific texts is the article. Articles are articles that explain a certain idea based on evidence.

What is the article used for?

  • To give information on any subject, written by experts in the field of article, to express an opinion and to defend this idea ,is an article of a certain length, title, usually published in newspapers and magazines that are signed for the purpose of proving.

Can articles be written on any subject?

  • Various methods such as witness demonstration, sampling, comparison can be used in the article.There is no subject limitation for article writing, it can be written on any topic.

Can anyone write an article?

  • Anyone who knows how to read and write can write articles. But the important thing is to write quality articles.Quality article; contains the right information, contains the answer the reader is looking for, is so fluent that it does not bore the reader and does not repeat words,spelling and punctuation are neat content that is not incorrect.
